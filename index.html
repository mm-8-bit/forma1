In this hands-on wax carving workshop, you’ll explore tools and techniques to create a one-of-a-kind jewelry piece. Beginners are welcome, no prior experience is necessary.
Couples ring experiences, private one-on-one sessions, group gatherings, and corporate workshops are available – click to explore all workshop experiences

1. Concept & Form

Translate your ideas from paper to wax. In this 2–3 hour session, you will carve a custom jewelry piece, focusing on the organic textures and shapes that surface during the creative process. This workshop is fully portable and can be hosted at a location of your choosing (travel fees applicable).

2. Transformation into Metal

Following our session, your wax sculpture is sent to be professionally transitioned into the metal of your choice, immortalizing every hand-carved detail and texture. As each piece is unique, casting fees are independent of the workshop and vary based on the final weight and material; an estimate will be provided during your session to guide your selection.

3. Back in Your Hands

Within two weeks, your design returns to you in its final form—carefully refined, polished, and ready to wear. This marks the end of the creative process, leaving you with a lasting, wearable piece of your own artistry.

Studio Offerings: A collection of workshops designed to bring your ideas to life.

The Duo Session: A ring making experience for couples.

An intimate space for two to translate their connection into a pair of handcrafted rings. Each participant carves a wax piece, producing distinct forms that emerge from a shared process.

The Private Dialogue: A dedicated one-on-one mentorship.


Explore the art of ring-making with focused, individual attention. This private session is tailored to your pace as you carve a custom ring—perfectly suited for your own hand or as a thoughtful gift for someone else.

The Collective Table: A shared ring-making experience.

A creative space for groups to gather and celebrate together. Ideal for birthdays, bridal showers, or private milestones, guests carve their own custom rings to mark the occasion.

The Object Study: An expansion of the craft.

A session dedicated to the exploration of different jewelry forms. From pendants to earrings and brooches, this workshop invites you to carve wax into a custom piece—shaping your own ideas into a wearable object.

The Executive: A jewelry-making experience for corporate teams.

An immersive workshop tailored for professional groups seeking a meaningful way to connect. This session invites participants to explore the meditative craft of wax carving. By designing and shaping custom pieces, your team will engage in a creative process that encourages fresh perspectives, problem-solving, and strengthened professional bonds through a focused, tactile experience.
